Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-02. This policy explains what information Casa Princeton Apartments collects through this website and how we use it.

1. Who we are

Casa Princeton Apartments ("we", "us", "our") operates the website at casaprincetonflorida.com and the apartment community located at 12835 SW 246 Terr, Homestead, FL 33032. For questions about this policy, contact hello@casaprincetonflorida.com or (786) 977-3672.

2. Information we collect

When you fill out forms on this site (contact, tour request, pricing alerts, newsletter, or exit-intent offer), we collect the information you provide directly:

  • Name (first and last)
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Floor plan or unit interest
  • Message content and move-in timing

We also automatically collect basic usage data through cookies and analytics tools (Google Analytics 4, Meta Pixel): pages visited, referring URL, approximate location (city-level), device type, and aggregate engagement metrics. We do not collect precise geolocation, payment information, or biometric data through this website.

3. How we use your information

  • To respond to your inquiries and schedule tours
  • To send you the information you requested (pricing, availability, newsletter)
  • To improve our website and marketing campaigns based on aggregate analytics
  • To comply with legal obligations (tax records, fair-housing recordkeeping, court orders)

4. How we share your information

We share data only with vendors that help us operate this site:

  • Web3Forms — receives form submissions and forwards them to our leasing email
  • Google Analytics 4 / Google Ads — measures site usage and ad performance
  • Meta (Facebook) Pixel — measures ad performance for Facebook/Instagram campaigns
  • Our property management software — to track leads and prospects

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties.

5. Cookies and tracking

This site uses cookies for analytics, advertising attribution, and to remember preferences. You can disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work correctly. We honor "Do Not Track" signals where required by law.

6. Your rights (Florida residents and beyond)

Subject to applicable law, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correct — ask us to fix inaccurate information
  • Delete — request deletion of your information (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt out — unsubscribe from marketing emails using the link at the bottom of every email

To exercise any of these rights, email hello@casaprincetonflorida.com with the subject line "Privacy Request". We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data retention

We retain inquiry data for 24 months after your last contact with us, unless you become a resident (in which case retention follows our resident records policy and applicable law).

8. Children's privacy

This site is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The effective date at the top of this page indicates when the current version took effect.
