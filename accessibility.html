— Accessibility —

We're committed to access for all.

Casa Princeton is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We continually improve the experience for everyone and apply the relevant accessibility standards.

Conformance status

The Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) define requirements for designers and developers to improve accessibility for people with disabilities. They define three levels of conformance: Level A, Level AA, and Level AAA. casaprincetonflorida.com is partially conformant with WCAG 2.1 level AA. "Partially conformant" means that some parts of the content do not fully conform to the accessibility standard.

Measures we take

  • Semantic HTML structure (proper headings, landmarks, lists)
  • Keyboard navigability for all interactive elements
  • "Skip to main content" link at the top of every page
  • Sufficient color contrast for text and meaningful UI
  • Descriptive alt text for informational images (rolling out alongside our photo updates)
  • ARIA labels on icon-only buttons and form controls
  • Resizable text without loss of content (up to 200%)

Known limitations

Despite our best efforts, some content may not yet be fully accessible:

  • Some decorative graphics use background CSS that does not announce a meaningful description to screen readers — these are intentionally decorative and marked aria-hidden="true".
  • Floor plan SVGs are diagrammatic; we are working on text-equivalent descriptions for each plan.
  • Embedded third-party content (Google Maps) follows the accessibility implementation of those vendors.

Physical accessibility (community)

Casa Princeton is designed to comply with the Americans with Disabilities Act (ADA) and the Fair Housing Act. The community offers accessible parking, ramps at primary entrances, accessible amenity areas, and a portion of units designed for persons with mobility or hearing impairments. To request a tour of an accessible unit, contact our leasing team.

Reporting an accessibility issue

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of casaprincetonflorida.com or our community. Please let us know if you encounter accessibility barriers:

  • Email: hello@casaprincetonflorida.com (subject line: "Accessibility")
  • Phone: (786) 977-3672
  • Mail: 12835 SW 246 Terr, Homestead, FL 33032

We aim to respond to feedback within 5 business days.

Compatibility

casaprincetonflorida.com is designed to be compatible with the following assistive technologies: NVDA and JAWS screen readers on Windows, VoiceOver on macOS and iOS, and TalkBack on Android. The site supports the latest two major versions of Chrome, Edge, Firefox, and Safari.
