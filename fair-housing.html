Equal Housing Opportunity

Casa Princeton Apartments fully complies with the Federal Fair Housing Act. We pledge to never discriminate in the rental of housing.

Equal Housing Opportunity

Casa Princeton Apartments operates in accordance with the Fair Housing Act and Equal Credit Opportunity Act.

Our policy

Casa Princeton Apartments does not discriminate against any person because of race, color, religion, national origin, sex, familial status, disability, age, marital status, sexual orientation, gender identity, or any other characteristic protected by federal, state of Florida, or Miami-Dade County law.

What this means in practice

  • We use the same screening criteria for every applicant: identity verification, income (typically 2.5–3× monthly rent), rental history, and standard credit/background screening.
  • We do not steer applicants toward or away from specific buildings, floors, or units based on protected characteristics.
  • Reasonable accommodations and modifications are provided to qualified applicants and residents with disabilities, in accordance with the Fair Housing Act.
  • Service animals and assistance animals (including emotional support animals with proper documentation) are exempt from pet fees and breed restrictions.
  • Marketing imagery, advertising, and language are reviewed to ensure they reflect the diversity of our community and welcome all qualified applicants.

Reasonable accommodations

If you have a disability and need a reasonable accommodation in the application process, lease terms, or use of the community, please contact our leasing team. Accommodations may include:

  • Alternative communication formats (large print, audio)
  • Accessible tour scheduling
  • Modifications to common-area access (subject to law)
  • Service animal and assistance animal accommodations

How to report a Fair Housing violation

If you believe you have experienced housing discrimination at Casa Princeton or anywhere else, you have the right to file a complaint:

  • U.S. Department of Housing and Urban Development (HUD): 1-800-669-9777 · hud.gov/fairhousing
  • Florida Commission on Human Relations: 1-800-342-8170 · fchr.myflorida.com
  • Casa Princeton internal contact: hello@casaprincetonflorida.com · (786) 977-3672

Casa Princeton is committed to providing housing opportunities to qualified applicants without regard to protected status. The official HUD Equal Housing Opportunity logo will replace the placeholder above once the official asset is available.
