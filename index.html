Frequently asked.

Don't see your question? Tap Contact Us — we reply within minutes.

What floor plans does Casa Princeton offer?

We currently offer One Bedroom (from $1,845/mo) and Two Bedroom (from $1,995/mo) apartments. Click here to view floor plans.

What are the starting prices on One Bedroom apartments?

Our One Bedroom starts at $1,845 base rent (12-month lease). Estimated total monthly cost around $1,980 — base rent plus mandatory monthly fees (trash $20, water/sewer $55, 1 GB internet $60). Fees billed monthly in addition to rent.

What are the starting prices on Two Bedroom apartments?

Our Two Bedroom starts at $1,995 base rent (12-month lease). Estimated total monthly cost around $2,140 — base rent plus mandatory monthly fees (trash $20, water/sewer $65, 1 GB internet $60). Fees billed monthly in addition to rent.

What's included in my monthly rent?

Base rent covers your home. Trash service ($20), water & sewer ($55–85), and 1 GB fiber internet ($60) are billed monthly in addition. Pet rent applies if you have pets.

What lease terms are available?

12-month leases qualify for our current 8 weeks free rent promotion (lease by May 31st). Other lease terms may be available — ask our leasing team.

Is there an application fee?

$49 per applicant covers credit, background, and identity verification, plus a $275 administrative fee per household.

Are pets allowed?

Yes — up to 2 pets per home. $250 one-time fee plus $20/mo per pet. Breed restrictions apply. Service and emotional-support animals are exempt from fees.

Do you offer covered parking?

Open parking is included for all residents. Reserved covered spots are $100/mo (limited availability). EV charging stations are available on-site, billed by usage.

When can I move in?

Most homes are available within 14–30 days of application approval. Some plans are immediately move-in ready — contact us for current availability.

Do I need renter's insurance?

Yes. All residents are required to carry minimum liability renter's insurance. Affordable plans are available — we can recommend providers at lease signing.
